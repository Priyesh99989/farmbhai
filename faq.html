🌾 General Questions

🤔 What is FarmDirect?
FarmDirect is a platform that connects customers directly with farmers. We take ZERO profit on rice - 82% of your payment goes to farmers.
💰 How does FarmDirect make money?
We take ZERO profit on rice. Delivery charges cover actual transportation and packaging costs only. This is a social impact project, not a profit-making business.
🚚 How long does delivery take?
Delivery usually takes 2-5 business days. You can track your order status on the Track Order page.
📍 Where do you deliver?
Currently we deliver only in Hyderabad. We are expanding to more cities soon.

📦 Order Questions

❌ How can I cancel my order?
Go to Dashboard → Find your order → Click "Cancel Order". Orders can be cancelled within 2 hours of placement.
📄 How do I get an invoice?
Go to Dashboard → Find your order → Click "Invoice" button. You can print or save as PDF.
⭐ Can I rate products?
Yes! After your order is delivered, you can rate the product from your Dashboard (1-5 stars).
🔔 Will I get order updates?
Yes, you will receive WhatsApp confirmation and email invoice. You can also check order status on Track Order page.

👨‍🌾 Farmer Questions

🌾 How do I register as a farmer?
Contact us at +91 6302588948 or support@farmdirect.com. We will add you to our farmer network.
💰 When do farmers get paid?
Farmers receive 82% of order value within 7 days of delivery confirmation.
