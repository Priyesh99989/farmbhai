Information We Collect

FarmDirect collects the following information from our users:

  • Personal Information: Name, phone number, delivery address, email address
  • Order Information: Products purchased, quantity, total amount, order status
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, device type

How We Use Your Information

  • To process and deliver your orders
  • To communicate order updates and status changes
  • To improve our products and services
  • To send order confirmations and invoices

Information Sharing

We share your information only with:

  • Farmers: They see your order to prepare produce (name, product, quantity)
  • Delivery Partners: They receive address and phone for delivery

We NEVER sell your personal information to third parties.

Data Security

We use Google Sheets with secure access controls. Your data is stored encrypted at rest. Only authorized personnel have access to customer data.

Your Rights

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of incorrect data
  • Request deletion of your account
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
