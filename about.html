🎯 Our Mission

To ensure farmers get fair price for their hard work while providing customers with fresh, quality produce at reasonable prices — with ZERO profit on rice.

📖 Our Story

FarmDirect was born from a simple observation: Farmers were getting only 30-40% of what customers paid. Middlemen took the rest. We decided to change that.

We connect customers directly with farmers. No middlemen. No hidden costs. No profit on rice.

💰 How It Works

1. You Order - Choose your product from our website.
2. Farmer Prepares - Farmer gets your order and harvests fresh produce.
3. Direct Delivery - We deliver directly from farm to your doorstep.
4. Farmer Gets Paid - 82% of your payment goes directly to farmer.

💚 Why Zero Profit?

Rice is a staple food. Everyone should have access to fair-priced, quality rice. We don't believe in making profit on essential food items. Our only revenue (if any) comes from delivery charges that cover actual costs.
